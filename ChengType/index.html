Current Works of ChengType

Chinese Airlines Aircraft Nationality & Registration Marks Font

Font for aircraft registration marks that are painted on part of aircrafts operated by some Chinese airlines (e.g., CHH, CES, and CYZ).

Airport Runway Designator Markings Font

Standard runway designator markings font in accordance with the requirements of ICAO Annex 14 Volume Ⅰ issued by the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) and Aerodrome Technical Standards (MH 5001-2021) issued by the Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC), containing digits 0～9 and letters “L”, “C” and “R”.

Other letters with similar characteristics are observed at some airports in New Zealand (e.g., NZAA) that are used as aircraft stand identification markings and taxiway markings. Further work needs to be done to complete the whole alphabet.
